Our Journey

Established in 1999, the Andhra Pradesh Housing Corporation has been at the forefront of addressing the housing needs of the state's growing population. What began as a modest initiative to provide affordable housing has evolved into a comprehensive program that has transformed the lives of hundreds of thousands of families.

Over the years, we have successfully implemented numerous housing schemes, including the flagship Indiramma Housing Program, which has provided homes to economically weaker sections of society. Our projects span across urban and rural areas, ensuring that quality housing is accessible to all segments of the population.

Today, we continue to innovate and adapt to changing needs, incorporating sustainable building practices, modern amenities, and smart city concepts into our housing projects. Our commitment remains unwavering - to ensure that every family in Andhra Pradesh has access to a safe, affordable, and dignified home.
