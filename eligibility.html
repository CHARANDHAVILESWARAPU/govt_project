Eligibility Criteria

Check if you qualify for our housing schemes and understand the requirements for application.

Income Criteria

Economically Weaker Section (EWS)

Annual household income up to ₹3 Lakhs

Subsidy: Up to ₹2.5 Lakhs

Lower Income Group (LIG)

Annual household income ₹3-6 Lakhs

Subsidy: Up to ₹2 Lakhs

Middle Income Group (MIG-I)

Annual household income ₹6-12 Lakhs

Interest subsidy available

Family Criteria

Age Requirement

Applicant must be 18 years or above

Family Ownership

No family member should own a pucca house

Previous Beneficiary

Should not have availed any central/state housing scheme

Required Documents

Identity Proof

Aadhar Card (mandatory)

Income Proof

Salary certificate, Income tax returns, or Income certificate

Bank Details

Bank account details and passbook copy

Other Documents

Caste certificate (if applicable), Property documents

Special Categories

Priority Categories

SC/ST, Minorities, Differently-abled, Women, Senior Citizens

Additional Benefits

Higher subsidy amounts and priority in allotment

Reservation

As per government norms and state policies

  • Subsidy up to ₹2.5 Lakhs
  • Interest subsidy on home loans
  • For EWS, LIG, and MIG categories
Active

AP Housing Scheme

State government housing initiative

  • Free housing for EWS families
  • Basic amenities included
  • Priority for SC/ST/Minorities

  • Subsidy up to ₹1.2 Lakhs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum income limit for EWS category?

The maximum annual household income for Economically Weaker Section (EWS) is ₹3 Lakhs per annum.

Can I apply if I already own a house?

No, if you or any family member owns a pucca house anywhere in India, you are not eligible for the housing scheme.

What documents are required for application?

Required documents include Aadhar Card, income proof, bank account details, caste certificate (if applicable), and property documents.

How long does the application process take?

The application review process typically takes 15-30 days. You will receive updates via SMS and email.
