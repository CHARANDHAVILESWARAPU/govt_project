How to Apply for Housing

Follow these simple steps to apply for your dream home through our government housing scheme.

1

Register Online

Fill out the online registration form with your personal details and documents.

2

Verify Documents

Upload required documents and verify your identity through OTP.

3

Pay Application Fee

Complete the payment process through our secure payment gateway.

4

Track Application

Monitor your application status and receive updates via SMS and email.
